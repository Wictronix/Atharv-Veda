What is Cancer?

Cancer is a life-threatening disease that starts when abnormal cells grow uncontrollably in any part of the body. These cells invade surrounding tissues and can spread to other areas, disrupting vital bodily functions. The condition may result from genetic changes, unhealthy lifestyles, exposure to harmful substances, and environmental factors.

In many cases, cancer goes undetected in its early stages due to mild or no symptoms. It often comes to attention when it reaches an advanced stage. Conventional treatments like chemotherapy, radiation, and surgery focus on removing or destroying cancer cells but can also harm healthy tissues, leading to side effects and weakened immunity.

Ayurveda provides a supportive and natural way to manage cancer through herbal remedies, detoxification, immune-boosting therapies, and a balanced lifestyle. The Ayurvedic approach strengthens the body’s natural defense system and targets the root cause of cancer without causing harm to healthy cells. At Karma Ayurveda, cancer treatment is focused on improving the patient’s overall health, reducing symptoms, and enhancing quality of life naturally.

What Causes Cancer?

  • Cancer is often linked to heredity. It happens when the genes that control cell growth change, causing abnormal cells to multiply and divide uncontrollably, which disrupts normal body functions.
  • Eating foods high in fat or sugar increases the risk of many types of cancer. Lack of physical activity is also a contributing factor.
  • Smoking cigarettes, cigars, or using e-cigarettes raises the risk of lung, pancreatic, esophageal, and oral cancers.
  • Exposure to harmful environmental toxins like radon, pesticides, and asbestos can also lead to cancer.
  • People assigned female at birth (AFAB) who use hormone replacement therapy may have a higher chance of developing breast and endometrial cancers.

Signs & Symptoms

  • Lumps or nodules under the skin that do not go away.
  • Persistent pain that lasts long.
  • Fever that usually gets worse in the evening.
  • Changes in skin, especially new or changing moles.
  • Unexplained weight loss.
  • Constant tiredness or fatigue.
  • Bleeding or bruising easily without injury.
  • Difficulty breathing.
  • Trouble swallowing food.

Ayurvedic Cancer Treatment Approach

Ayurvedic cancer treatment focuses on improving the body’s immunity, cleansing toxins, and creating an environment that is unfavorable for cancer cells to grow. The treatment does not aim to destroy cells with force but works gently to restore the body's internal balance.
