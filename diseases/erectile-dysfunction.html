What is Erectile Dysfunction?

Erectile Dysfunction (ED) is a condition where a man is unable to achieve or maintain an erection firm enough for sexual intercourse. It is commonly referred to as impotence. This happens when the hormonal and nervous systems don’t send proper signals during sexual arousal. At Atharveda, Ayurvedic herbs for erectile dysfunction are used to address the root causes naturally.
It’s normal to occasionally face difficulty in getting an erection, but if it becomes frequent or long-lasting, it may indicate a deeper health issue. ED can cause emotional stress, low confidence, and may even impact relationships and daily life. It can also be an early sign of heart-related problems, making timely treatment important.

What Causes Erectile Dysfunction?

Many people searching for natural solutions may look for terms like male erection Ayurvedic medicine or best Ayurvedic treatment for erectile dysfunction. However, self-medication is not recommended. It’s important to consult an expert who can identify the root cause.
Common causes include:

  • Diabetes: This affects blood vessels and nerves, both crucial for achieving an erection. Diabetes-related nerve damage, known as peripheral neuropathy, is a major contributor to ED.
  • Depression and Medications: Mental health issues, especially depression, are closely linked with ED. Antidepressant drugs may also lead to erectile issues. Since depression, heart disease, and ED are often connected, a full evaluation is necessary.
  • Neurological Problems: Conditions like multiple sclerosis, spinal cord injuries, or surgeries in the pelvic area may damage nerves. Excessive alcohol use and exposure to toxins also affect nerve function, causing ED.
